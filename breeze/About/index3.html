About Breeze 2k15

The wind is in your sails! The moment has finally arrived for you to unleash your inner prowess and take

the world by storm!

For all you novice photographers, peppy fashionistas and in-the-closet Picasso, it’s your time to shine.

Whether it’s remembering the in details of fandom you excel at or vociferously debating a current issue

; be it your passion to sing or show people how your body grooves, we’ve got it all. This time, the

Spotlight will be on you at SHIV NADAR UNIVERSITY’S annual inter-college sports-techno-cultural fest:

BREEZE!

SHIV NADAR UNIVERSITY is a multi-disciplinary, student centric, research-focused university offering a

full range of academic programs at the undergraduate, post-graduate and doctoral level.

Located on a 286-acre campus in India’s National Capital region, SNU is a private philanthropic

institution established by the Shiv Nadar Foundation in 2011. With its multi-disciplinary

curriculum providing students a strong foundation in wide array of disciplines: the humanities

ad social science , natural science, technology and engineering studies , communication and

management, SNU has been set-up with a vision of becoming one of the best universities in the

world – India’s very own Ivy League

BREEZE’15 features events ranging from the genres of photography, art and quizzing to debating, music

and sports. No matter what kindles your inner fire, Breeze has something for you to fan those

flames higher!

Join this 96-hour long party with over 15 colleges from Delhi-NCR region, hosting over 5000 students. If

the quality of events hasn’t been incentive enough, there are prizes worth over 2 lakh rupees up

for grabs! Free your schedule cause the East wind’s coming your way.

See you at BREEZE’15: 22 October – 24 October
